# Legal Safety & Content Compliance Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how SustainHuman content is designed to be legally safe and avoid potential complaints or liability issues.

## Legal Protections Implemented

### 1. Clear Disclaimers
✅ **Every article page includes a prominent disclaimer box** stating:
- Content is educational and informational only
- Not medical, health, financial, or professional advice
- Users should consult qualified professionals
- No guarantees of results
- User assumes all responsibility

✅ **Footer disclaimer on main page** reinforcing educational nature

### 2. Safe Content Approach

#### What We DO:
✅ Provide general educational information
✅ Cite that research "has explored" or "studies suggest"
✅ Use phrases like "may," "might," "can," "potential"
✅ Acknowledge individual variation
✅ Direct readers to consult professionals
✅ Present multiple perspectives
✅ Stick to well-established, non-controversial science

#### What We AVOID:
❌ Specific medical advice or diagnoses
❌ Prescriptive health recommendations
❌ Claims of cures or guaranteed results
❌ Unproven or pseudoscientific claims
❌ Financial advice or investment recommendations
❌ Legal advice
❌ Controversial political statements
❌ Copyrighted content without permission
❌ Defamatory statements about people or companies
❌ Dangerous or risky recommendations

### 3. Content Safety Principles

#### Medical/Health Content
- Always general and educational
- Never diagnose or treat conditions
- Always recommend consulting healthcare providers
- Acknowledge individual differences
- Use cautious language ("may help," "research suggests")

#### Environmental/Climate Content
- Stick to scientific consensus
- Avoid political advocacy
- Present factual information
- Acknowledge complexity and uncertainty
- Focus on individual actions, not policy prescriptions

#### Technology/Product Content
- Provide general information, not endorsements
- Encourage independent research
- Mention privacy and security considerations
- Avoid specific product recommendations
- No affiliate relationships or undisclosed conflicts

### 4. Image Usage
Current images use Unsplash URLs which provide:
- Free to use images
- No attribution required (though appreciated)
- Commercial use allowed

**Important:** If you add your own images:
- Only use images you own or have rights to use
- Use stock photo sites with proper licenses
- Never copy images from Google Images without verification
- Keep records of image sources and licenses

### 5. Avoiding Copyright Issues

✅ **Safe Practices:**
- Write original content
- Paraphrase and cite sources when referencing research
- Use public domain or licensed images
- Link to external sources rather than copying content

❌ **Avoid:**
- Copying text from other websites
- Using copyrighted images without permission
- Reproducing charts/graphs without permission
- Extensive quoting without proper attribution

### 6. Avoiding Defamation

✅ **Safe Practices:**
- Discuss general concepts, not specific people/companies
- Stick to factual, verifiable information
- Use neutral, objective language
- Present multiple viewpoints

❌ **Avoid:**
- Negative statements about specific individuals
- Unverified claims about companies
- Personal attacks or character judgments
- Statements that could harm reputations

### 7. Privacy Considerations

✅ **Current Implementation:**
- No user data collection in current static site
- No cookies or tracking
- No user accounts or personal information storage

⚠️ **If You Add Features:**
- Newsletter: Use reputable email service, include privacy policy
- Contact form: Secure data transmission, clear privacy notice
- Analytics: Disclose tracking, provide opt-out
- Comments: Moderate for safety, have terms of service

## Content Review Checklist

Before publishing new content, verify:

- [ ] Content is educational and general, not prescriptive
- [ ] Includes appropriate disclaimers
- [ ] Recommends consulting professionals where appropriate
- [ ] Uses cautious, qualified language
- [ ] No specific medical/health/financial advice
- [ ] No copyrighted material without permission
- [ ] Images are properly licensed
- [ ] No defamatory statements
- [ ] Factually accurate and verifiable
- [ ] Acknowledges limitations and individual variation
- [ ] No dangerous or risky recommendations
- [ ] No political advocacy or controversial positions

## If You Receive a Complaint

1. **Take it seriously** - Don't ignore legal notices
2. **Document everything** - Save all communications
3. **Consult a lawyer** - For any legal threats or demands
4. **Consider removal** - If content is questionable, removing it may be safest
5. **Respond professionally** - Be courteous and cooperative

## Recommended Additions

### Privacy Policy
If you collect any user data (emails, contact forms, analytics), create a privacy policy explaining:
- What data you collect
- How you use it
- How you protect it
- User rights
- How to contact you

### Terms of Service
Consider adding terms that:
- Reiterate disclaimers
- Limit liability
- Explain acceptable use
- Reserve right to modify content

### About/Contact Page
Include:
- Clear contact information
- Transparency about who runs the site
- Mission and values

## Resources

- Consult with a lawyer for specific legal advice
- Review FTC guidelines for online content
- Check local laws regarding health/medical content
- Consider professional liability insurance if providing advice

## Remember

**The safest approach is always:**
1. Be educational, not prescriptive
2. Include clear disclaimers
3. Direct people to professionals
4. Use cautious, qualified language
5. Stick to well-established facts
6. When in doubt, leave it out

This website is designed to be informational and inspire learning, not to replace professional guidance in any field.
