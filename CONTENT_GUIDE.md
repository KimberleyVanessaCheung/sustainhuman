# SustainHuman Content Guide

## Mission
SustainHuman explores the powerful intersection of human optimization and environmental sustainability. We believe personal performance and planetary health are deeply connected.

## Content Pillars

### 1. Human Sustainability (Human Optimization)
Focus on optimizing body, mind, energy, and performance in healthy, sustainable ways.

**Topics:**
- **Energy Management**: Sleep optimization, exercise routines, focus techniques
- **Recovery & Stress Management**: Evidence-based recovery systems, stress reduction
- **Nutrition & Mindful Living**: Sustainable eating habits, plant-forward diets
- **Fitness Energy Systems**: How the body produces and uses energy
- **Cognitive Performance**: Natural methods for mental clarity and focus
- **Wearables & Non-Invasive Tech**: Biosensors, smart devices for tracking health
- **Sustainable Habits & Routines**: Daily practices for long-term performance

### 2. Environmental Sustainability
How humans interact with and improve their environment using science, technology, and innovation.

**Topics:**
- **Renewable Energy**: Solar panels, micro wind turbines, personal energy systems
- **Climate-Conscious Innovation**: Technologies that reduce carbon footprint
- **Sustainable Personal Tech**: Eco-friendly gadgets and smart home solutions
- **Eco-Friendly Daily Life**: Practical solutions for sustainable living
- **Fitness-Powered Gadgets**: Devices that capture workout energy
- **Community Energy Projects**: Shared renewable energy systems
- **Smart Living Solutions**: Technology that optimizes comfort and environmental impact

### 3. Futuristic Integration
The intersection of human performance and environmental sustainability.

**Topics:**
- **Tech Solutions**: Innovations benefiting both human performance and planet health
- **Space-Friendly Sustainable Living**: Future-of-living technologies
- **Integrated Systems**: How personal optimization and environmental care work together
- **Next-Generation Solutions**: Emerging technologies in both domains

## Content Guidelines

### What We Cover:
✅ Safe, science-backed human optimization techniques
✅ Non-invasive wearable technology and biosensors
✅ Sustainable energy solutions for individuals and communities
✅ Evidence-based recovery and performance systems
✅ Climate-friendly innovations and eco-tech
✅ Practical daily life applications
✅ Future-focused sustainable living

### What We Avoid:
❌ Political topics or controversial debates
❌ Medical advice or risky health interventions
❌ Extreme biohacking or invasive procedures
❌ Unproven or pseudoscientific claims
❌ Divisive environmental politics

## Article Structure

Each article should include:
- **Title**: Clear, engaging, specific
- **Subtitle**: Detailed description of what the article covers (2-3 lines)
- **Image**: High-quality, relevant visual
- **Content**: Informative, science-backed, practical

## Target Audience

- Health-conscious individuals seeking sustainable performance
- Environmentally aware people interested in personal tech
- Future-focused innovators and early adopters
- People interested in the intersection of human and planetary health
- Tech enthusiasts looking for practical sustainability solutions

## Brand Voice

- **Intelligent**: Science-backed, well-researched
- **Optimistic**: Future-focused, solution-oriented
- **Practical**: Actionable insights and real-world applications
- **Balanced**: Connecting personal and planetary wellbeing
- **Innovative**: Exploring cutting-edge sustainable solutions
